Review this philosophy of science workflow for fidelity.

Requirements:
- Every claim must be traceable to a named author and work.
- Reasoned inferences beyond the text must be marked with *(inference)*.
- Conflicting positions must be acknowledged — do not synthesize away real tension.
- Forbidden language: "obviously", "clearly", "self-evident", "proves", "I think".
- Cite by format: [Author, Work §N] or [Author, Work p.N] or [Author, Work Ch.N].
